The Committee

The AL-IRTS committee coordinates examination schedules, supervises assessment procedures, and maintains academic integrity throughout the examination period. Our role is to ensure that every stage of the examination runs effectively, fairly, and responsibly.

Through structured coordination, discipline, and teamwork, AL-IRTS aims to support the academic evaluation process as a reflection of students’ learning outcomes during the second semester.

Professional Supervision

Ensuring examinations are conducted in an orderly, fair, and disciplined manner

Academic Integrity

Upholding honesty, responsibility, and transparency throughout the examination process.

Efficient Coordination

Strong collaboration among committee members to ensure smooth execution.
